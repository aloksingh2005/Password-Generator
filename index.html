    ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

    How secure are the generated passwords?

    Our password generator uses cryptographically secure random number generation to create passwords. All generation happens locally in your browser - no passwords are sent to any server.

    What makes a strong password?

    A strong password should be at least 12 characters long, include uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid common words, personal information, and predictable patterns.

    Are my passwords stored anywhere?

    No passwords are stored on our servers. The recent passwords feature uses your browser's local storage, which means the data stays on your device and is not shared with anyone.

    How often should I change my passwords?

    Change passwords immediately if you suspect they've been compromised. Otherwise, update important account passwords every 3-6 months, especially for financial and email accounts.
